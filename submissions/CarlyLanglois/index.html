Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • rice paper wrappers
  • rice noodles
  • tofu
  • carrots
  • lettuce
  • green onions

Instructions

Shred carrots and lettuce. Toss in large bowl with cooked rice noodles. Cook tofu to your preference (ie bake/saute) and add to bowl with other ingredients. Wet rice paper wrapper with warm water. Place a scoop of filling onto wet wrapper and roll tightly. Repeat until all of filling is rolled up.

Dipping Sauce

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • chili garlic paste
  • lime juice
  • chopped fresh ginger
  • honey, or alternative sweetener
  • coconut oil

Instructions

Combine appropriate amounts of each ingredient to taste. If too salty, add more honey (or alternative.) If too acidic, add more oil. If too spicy, add sweetener or thin out with water.

Kiwi/Lime Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

  • oreos
  • coconut milk

Filling

  • cashews, soaked for 2-4 hours and drained
  • avocados
  • honey (or alternative sweetener)
  • lime juice
  • lemon juice
  • kiwi slices

Instructions

For crust, pulse oreos and coconut milk together in food processor until fine sand texture. Push mixture into the bottom of a springform tin and chill. For filling, combine ingredients into food processor until uniform and creamy. Pour onto chilled crust. Top with kiwi slices. Serve chilled.

Pina Coladas

Ingredients

  • pineapple juice
  • cream of coconut
  • rum

Instructions

Combine ingredients in blender. Serve over ice.
